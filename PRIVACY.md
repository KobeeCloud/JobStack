## 6. Your Rights Under RODO/GDPR

You have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right of Access (Article 15 GDPR)
Request a copy of all data we hold about you.

**How:** Email privacy@jobstack.pl with subject "RODO: Access Request"

**Timeline:** We respond within **30 days** (or 90 days for complex requests)

### 6.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16 GDPR)
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

**How:** Update in your account settings or email us

### 6.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17 GDPR)
Request deletion of your data.

**How:** Delete your account or email privacy@jobstack.pl

**Exceptions:** We may retain data if:
- Required by law (e.g., tax records)
- Necessary for legal claims
- Public interest

### 6.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18 GDPR)
Limit how we use your data.

**Example:** Stop email alerts but keep account

### 6.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20 GDPR)
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON, CSV).

**How:** Email privacy@jobstack.pl with subject "RODO: Data Export"

### 6.6 Right to Object (Article 21 GDPR)
Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

**Example:** Object to analytics tracking

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7(3) GDPR)
If we process data based on consent, you can withdraw it anytime.

**Effect:** We stop that processing immediately

### 6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
If you believe we violated RODO, you can complain to:
